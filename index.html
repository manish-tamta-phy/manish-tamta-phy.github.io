General Overview

Hey! Welcome to my personal webpage. Here I post things related to Science, in particular Physics. My name is Manish Kumar Tamta, I'm 20 years old. Currently I am doing MS physics from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Mohali. I did my undergrad viz. B.Sc. (H) Physics from St. Stephen's College, University of Delhi. I did a summer school at Inter University Centre for Astronomy and Astrophysics (IUCAA) Pune. As of now I am interested in Astrophysics and Cosmology. In order to gain experience I am doing a research project at the Australian National University in the same field.

The motivation behind this webpage is that I want to share my experiences, my understanding of the subject and some references so that some needful people could get benefit.
