Globular clusters

Q. How many globular clusters are there in Milky Way?

A. Around 150, a few more are likely to exist, hidden behind the thick disc of our galaxy.

Q. What causes a tidal shock?

A. A tidal shock occurs whenever the orbit of a cluster takes it through the plane of a galaxy.
